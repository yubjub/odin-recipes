Fried Chicken Recipe

the steps to making some good old fried chicken that everyone know and loves are actually quite easy and without further ado let me show you the number one fried chicken recipes that can even rival the likes of kfc!

ingredients

  • chicken legs and thighs
  • flour
  • eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • Yubjubseasoning mix

    • Steps

    1. Grab a bowl and mix in flour and some yubjub seasoning
    2. Crack 5 eggs in a separate bowl
    3. Grab each piece of your lovely chicken and give it an even coating of flour then of egg
    4. Heat up your frying pan with the oil until it reaches 300 degrees
    5. Gently drop your chicken pieces into the hot oil bath till nice and crisy or with internal temp of 180 degrees
    6. Carfully remove your golden brown fried chicken and place them on a cooling rack for consumption
