have you ever felt that a dish you were preparing was missing something? you know it needs more seasoning but the hassle of finding which mix complements your dish could be a real pain at times.

making yubjub seasoning is as simple as can get. And best of all its one solution that trumps alll your food needs.

ingredients

  • paprika
  • lemon pepper
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 3 schleems
  • 1 grumbo
  • rosemary

mix them all together and voila, the perfect seasoning for practically every dish
